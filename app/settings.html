Quick Tip for Faster Updates

To ensure you're viewing the latest version of the site after an update, please open your browser's Developer Tools and disable caching. Here's how:

  • Chrome: Press F12 or Ctrl + Shift + I, go to the Network tab, and check "Disable cache."
  • Firefox: Press F12 or Ctrl + Shift + I, go to the Network tab, and check "Disable cache."
  • Safari: Press Cmd + Alt + I, go to the Network tab, and check "Disable cache."

This temporary step helps load the updated content directly from the server, bypassing the cache.
